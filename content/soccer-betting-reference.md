# Soccer (Football) — Rules & Sports Betting Reference

## Basic Rules

### Match Structure
- Two halves of 45 minutes each (90 minutes total)
- Stoppage/injury time added at the end of each half (determined by referee)
- Half-time break of 15 minutes
- A goal is scored when the entire ball crosses the goal line

### Players
- 11 players per side (including goalkeeper)
- Up to 3-5 substitutions allowed (depends on competition rules)
- A team cannot continue with fewer than 7 players

### Key Rules
- **Offside**: A player is offside if they are nearer to the opponent's goal line than both the ball and the second-to-last defender when the ball is played to them
- **Fouls**: Direct/indirect free kicks awarded for illegal challenges, handballs, etc.
- **Yellow card**: Caution (two yellows = red)
- **Red card**: Immediate ejection, team plays with 10 players
- **Penalty kick**: Awarded for fouls inside the 18-yard box
- **VAR (Video Assistant Referee)**: Used in major leagues to review goals, penalties, red cards, and mistaken identity

### Extra Time & Penalties (Knockout Matches Only)
- If tied after 90 minutes: two 15-minute extra time periods
- If still tied: penalty shootout (5 kicks each, then sudden death)
- **Important**: Regular season/league matches can end in a draw

## Competition Formats

### League (Round-Robin)
- Every team plays every other team home and away
- 3 points for a win, 1 for a draw, 0 for a loss
- Examples: Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, MLS

### Cup/Knockout
- Single elimination (sometimes two-legged ties with aggregate score)
- Examples: FA Cup, Champions League knockout stage, World Cup knockout stage

### Group + Knockout
- Group stage (round-robin in small groups) → knockout rounds
- Examples: World Cup, Champions League, Euro Championship

## Common Betting Markets

### Match Result (1X2)
- **1** = Home win, **X** = Draw, **2** = Away win
- This is the most common soccer bet globally
- Settles on 90 minutes + stoppage time (does NOT include extra time/penalties unless specified)

### Over/Under (Totals)
- Total goals scored in the match (e.g., Over 2.5 / Under 2.5)
- 2.5 is the most common line in soccer
- Can also apply to corners, cards, shots

### Both Teams to Score (BTTS)
- Yes/No — will both teams score at least one goal?

### Handicap / Spread
- **Asian Handicap**: Eliminates the draw by giving a team a fractional head start (e.g., -0.5, -1.5, -0.25)
- **European Handicap**: Includes the draw as an outcome (e.g., -1 with three-way result)

### Correct Score
- Predict the exact final score (high odds, hard to hit)

### First/Last/Anytime Goalscorer
- Which player scores first, last, or at any point

### Half-Time / Full-Time
- Predict the result at half-time AND at full-time (9 possible outcomes)

### Props / Specials
- Corners (over/under, team with most)
- Cards (total cards, player to be carded)
- Shots on target
- Player assists

### Live / In-Play
- Markets offered during the match with odds updating in real time

## Key Concepts for Betting Context

### Settlement Rules
- **90-minute rule**: Most markets settle on the result after 90 minutes + stoppage time. Extra time and penalties are typically separate markets.
- **Dead heat rules**: Apply when multiple players share a finish (e.g., top goalscorer)
- **Void/push**: If a player doesn't participate, goalscorer bets are typically void

### Match Abandonment
- If a match is abandoned before 90 minutes and not replayed within a defined window, bets may be voided
- Some books settle if a certain threshold is reached (e.g., 80+ minutes played)

### Postponements
- Bets typically stand if the match is rescheduled within 24-48 hours (varies by book)

### Own Goals
- Generally do NOT count for goalscorer markets
- DO count for over/under and match result markets

### VAR Impact
- Goals can be disallowed minutes after being scored
- Penalties can be awarded/overturned after review
- Affects in-play betting significantly

### Red Cards
- Drastically change match dynamics — a team down to 10 players is statistically much less likely to win
- Important for live betting adjustments
